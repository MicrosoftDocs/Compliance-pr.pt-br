## <a name="microsoft-and-nist-sp-800-171"></a><span data-ttu-id="8a657-115">Microsoft e NIST SP 800-171</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8a657-115">Microsoft and NIST SP 800-171</span></span>
